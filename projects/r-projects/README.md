## Projects

### 1. Operational Risk Briefing: Strategic Audit Prioritization
**Link:** [View on RPubs](https://rpubs.com/slammerjammer/1422063)

**Approach:**

**Key Outcomes:**

- Identified key risk indicators
- Created actionable audit schedule recommendations

**Skills Demonstrated:**

- Risk Analysis
- Data Visualization
